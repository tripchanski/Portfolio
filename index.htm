BlackLock

Most task management apps make the same critical mistake: they let users mark tasks as complete without actually doing them. It's too easy to lie to yourself with a simple checkbox. BlackLock solves this problem by requiring multiple confirmations before marking a task as complete. This deliberate friction makes you pause and think: "Maybe I should actually complete this task in reality?"
